About us

Vijay Vidya, is a Nursery & Primary School, established in the year 1971, under the aegis of Vijay Vidya Trust registered at Chennai. Vijay Vidya was established by Sri S Muthuvelu, a great Visionary and a Philanthropist in a very small way. With the support of public and the neighborhood, it is our pleasure to mention that we are marching towards to celebrate our Golden Anniversary.

Vijay Vidya is an English medium school which caters to the academic need of children in the age group of 2.5 years to 10 years – From PreK G to Fifth Standard.

Our school is recognized by Government of Tamil Nadu and our fees are approved by Tamil Nadu Fee Fixation Committee. We provide 25%admissions towards RTE. We enjoyan excellent reputation in our neighborhood.

Every of cur caring staff members identify each child’s hidden potential, lay a very strong foundation for them to grow as great citizen of our county. All our staff members are great pillars to our institution who have a very rich experience in moulding the young brains. Our school infrastructure includes well computer center, library etc. The school has acquired new and modern teaching infrastructure which includes interactive white boards and projectors.

About Founder

The Vijay Vidya Nursery & Primary School was a result of the realization of the vision of our founder S Muthuvelu. He was a great visionary, philanthropist, and established for the betterment of the education for the children of all kind in the locality. The beginning was very humble 50 years back, with just two teachers and three children! Over the years the school has grown with his focused attention and wife M Vijaya Lakshmi dedicated efforts. Vijay Vidya functions under the auspicious of Vijay Vidya Trust. School has contributed largely to the development of children's education in the locality. Located in Ramakrishna Nagar, between Raja Annamalai Puram and Mandaiveli, is one of the prominent locations in Chennai. Vijay Vidya Nursery and Primary School affiliated to the Board of Matriculation schools was started in the year 1971 with a view to cater to the educational needs of children. The school runs classes from Pre KG LKG to class V. The medium of instruction is English and the other languages taught are Tamil and Hindi.

School Emblem

The meaning of the school emblem is very significant. The meaning of the words "Knowledge is power" means that true power comes from knowledge. No individual or nation can prosper in life without knowledge.

The words " knowledge is power" inspires students to pursue education, as education alone has the power to bring to the forefront their hidden potential so as to leave their indelible mark in society.
