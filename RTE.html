RTE Admissions

In our School, the entry level class for Right To Education (RTE) is LKG. Number of seats available in the entry level class LKG is 60 children. We have allocated 25% of 60 seats exclusively for RTE Children, which is 15 children. We can admit upto 15 children under RTE Provisions / Act.

Parents seeking admissions under RTE provisions, are requested to submit your application only through online mode. Please visit http://rte.tnschools.qov.in/tamil-nadu and submit your application with required information. Parents will get admission information to their registered mobile number. Parents may approach school office with required documents, for any assistance in submitting the Online RTE Application

Website for RTE Admission, please visit http://rte.tnschools.qov.in/tamil-nadu
Start Date for submission of Online RTE application: 22.04.2019
Last date for submission of Online RTE Application: 18.05.2019

Documents Required for RTE Admissions ( Scan and keep as a file)

  • Income Certificate
  • Birth Certificate for a New admission
  • Passport size photographs 2(copies)
  • Aadhar Card
  • Community Certificate
