Fee Payment

School Management strictly follows the school fees fixed by the Fee Determination Committee. School fee may be paid by Cheque / Demand Draft / Cash. Payment other than cash may be drawn on Vijay Vidya Nursery School, Chennai 600028.

Parents must note that the school application fee and school fees once paid will NOT be refunded for any reasons. In the event of any unforeseen circumstances, parent intend to withdraw their ward from the school, should pay their full annual fee dues to get their Transfer Certificate for their ward.

Parent can choose to make the school fee either the full annual fee or by Term wise.
  • Term fees to be paid on or before June 30 (with fine upto July 15)
  • Term fees to be paid on or before September 30 (with fine upto Oct 15)
  • Term fees to be paid on or before January 31 (with fine upto Feb 15)

    • Payment can be made in cash before the due dates or by cheque drawn on M/s Vijay Vidya Nursery & Primary School, Chennai 600028
