Welcome to Our School

Vijay Vidya, is a Nursery & Primary School, established in the year 1971, under the aegis of Vijay Vidya Trust registered at Chennai. Vijay Vidya was established by Sri S Muthuvelu, a great Visionary and a Philanthropist in a very small way. With the support of public and the neighborhood, it is our pleasure to mention that we are marching towards to celebrate our Golden Anniversary.

Vijay Vidya is an English medium school which caters to the academic need of children in the age group of 2.5 years to 10 years – From PreK G to Fifth Standard.

Our school is recognized by Government of Tamil Nadu and our fees are approved by Tamil Nadu Fee Fixation Committee. We provide 25%admissions towards RTE. We enjoyan excellent reputation in our neighborhood.

Every of cur caring staff members identify each child’s hidden potential, lay a very strong foundation for them to grow as great citizen of our county. All our staff members are great pillars to our institution who have a very rich experience in moulding the young brains. Our school infrastructure includes well computer center, library etc. The school has acquired new and modern teaching infrastructure which includes interactive white boards and projectors.
